Real-time ISL recognition powered by MediaPipe and TensorFlow — running entirely on your device.

STEP 1
Allow camera access
Click "Start Prediction" and allow your browser to use the webcam. Nothing is uploaded — all processing happens live.
STEP 2
Choose a mode
Word mode spells letter-by-letter. Sentence mode records a short gesture and predicts a whole phrase at once. Switch anytime with M.
STEP 3
Sign and hold steady
Show the sign clearly in frame and hold it briefly — the progress bar at the top of the camera fills up as it confirms your sign.
STEP 4
Confirm and speak
Press Space to confirm a word, Enter to speak the sentence out loud, or C to clear. Common phrases on the right speak automatically when matched.
